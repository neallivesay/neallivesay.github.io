Welcome to my website! I am a postdoctoral research fellow in the Institute for Experiential AI at Northeastern University in Boston. I am interested in mathematics (e.g., matrices, graphs, geometry), computer science (e.g., high-performance computing, computer architecture), and machine learning.

I am the Principal Investigator for an American Institute of Mathematics SQuaRE, which is investigating moduli spaces of irregular singular differential operators.
