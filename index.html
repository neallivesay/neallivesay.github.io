Welcome to my website! I am a postdoctoral research fellow in the Institute for Experiential AI at Northeastern University. I currently reside in Boston.

I am the Principal Investigator for an American Institute of Mathematics SQuaRE, which is investigating the Deligne--Simpson problem and rigidity problem for irregular singular differential operators.
